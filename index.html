About Me

The Face Behind the Look

I'm Nisha Malhotra — a certified makeup artist based in Ahmedabad with over 8 years of experience crafting looks that celebrate individuality. Trained at the prestigious VLCC and MUA Academy in Mumbai, I bring both technical mastery and artistic intuition to every face I touch.

From intimate mehendi mornings to grand wedding ceremonies, I understand the emotional significance of each occasion. I specialize in looks that photograph beautifully, feel comfortable all day, and stay picture-perfect through every moment.
